# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

## In short

Be respectful. Assume good faith. Focus on the work.

## Unacceptable

- Personal attacks, harassment, discrimination.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Enforcement

Report incidents privately to **levendmohammadfr@gmail.com**. Maintainers will respond within 7 days. Consequences range from a warning to a ban, at maintainer discretion. Reports are kept confidential.
